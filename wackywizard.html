Wacky Wizard's tower defense

The assignment for this project was for us to build a game from scratch. I started with the idea to make a space invaders type of game, but i decided later on to go about it a different way.

These were the first sketches for my game, where the player had to click the goblins before they reach the line.

I later decided I wanted to change it so that the player had to click the goblin boss as fast as possible, before the timer runs out
